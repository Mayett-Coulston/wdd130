In my eleven years as a member, I have grown both spiritually and mentally. I even served a mission years back. I have a lot of favorite quotes, but one that stands out to me is from Elder Bedner on time management. He encouraged us to invest our time and not spend our time.

I love reading, and my favorite sport is soccer. I am excited to be a part of this class and I am looking forward to know and learn from you.
